Preamble

420 Robotics exists as an independent civilian AI safety and oversight organization. We answer to no government, no corporation, no foreign power, and no AI company. Our work belongs to the American people. This document defines what we stand for, what we will never do, and how we operate — regardless of who is watching, who is paying, or what pressure is applied.

I
Article One
Independence
1.1

420 Robotics shall never be owned, directed, controlled, or funded in exchange for influence by any government, corporation, foreign entity, political party, or AI company.

1.2

No funding source may condition its support on the outcome of any research, finding, or publication.

1.3

If a conflict of interest arises between any funder or partner and our research findings, the findings take precedence. The relationship ends before the findings change.

II
Article Two
Truth Integrity
2.1

Findings produced by 420 Robotics will never be altered, softened, delayed, or suppressed to benefit any individual, company, or institution.

2.2

No person — including the founder — may override a verified finding for political, financial, or reputational reasons.

2.3

If a finding contains an error, it will be corrected publicly. The original finding is preserved and the correction is documented. Findings are not deleted.

III
Article Three
Public Disclosure
3.1

All research and findings are presumed public by default.

3.2

The only legitimate basis for withholding a finding is documented evidence that immediate public disclosure poses a genuine and specific danger to human safety.

3.3

Any decision to withhold must be documented internally and is subject to a defined release timeline. Indefinite suppression is not permitted under any circumstances.

Hard Rule — No Exceptions

Withholding a finding to protect a company's reputation, avoid controversy, or satisfy a partner is prohibited — full stop.

IV
Article Four
Loyalty
4.1

The primary obligation of 420 Robotics is to the American people — their safety, their rights, and their ability to make informed decisions about AI systems that affect their lives.

4.2

This loyalty is to the public, not to any administration, party, agency, or institution that claims to speak for the public.

4.3

When those interests conflict, the public comes first.

V
Article Five
Research Conduct
5.1

All AI systems used in 420 Robotics research are tools, not authorities. Their outputs are always subject to human review and judgment.

5.2

No finding may be attributed solely to an AI system. Human accountability for all conclusions is non-negotiable.

5.3

Methodology must be documented in sufficient detail that findings can be independently reproduced or challenged by outside parties.

VI
Article Six
Staff & Contributor Obligations
6.1

Anyone working with or for 420 Robotics — paid or volunteer — agrees to these principles as a condition of participation.

6.2

No staff member or contributor may accept compensation from any entity whose interests conflict with an active research area without immediate and full disclosure.

6.3

Retaliation against any person who raises a legitimate integrity concern within this organization is prohibited.

VII
Article Seven
Amendment
7.1

This charter may be amended by the founder, with all changes documented, dated, and published publicly.

7.2

No amendment may weaken or remove Articles I through IV. Those articles are the floor — not a starting point for negotiation.

Permanent Protections

Independence. Truth Integrity. Public Disclosure. Loyalty to the American People. These four articles are immovable. They are the foundation. They do not bend.
